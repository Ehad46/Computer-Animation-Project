Story Steps

Introduction
Introduction of the character and his knowledge of his powers by using them so cassually and the way the corporation rules over society in extreme ways.

Problem
The kid is abducted by the corporation in order to use him for scientific porpurses. The protagonist tries to escape using his powers, going through walls, fighting robots, breaking walls.

Realization
He realices he is acting guided by his instincts against the society's rules, becoming a villain in his mind and everyone's else, deserving death.

Resolution
The character sees himelf as a terrorist and thinks of the posibility of coming back as a lab rat, but can't deal with that idea and decides to kill himself.
